Magento 2 Matrix Rates

Magento 2 Matrix Rates Extension lets you offer custom made shipping options. Use various custom variables or their combination to calculate the shipping rate.

The value of attributes like location, Item quantities, weight, etc. may vary from customer to customer. Depending on this fact, the customer will ask for varied shipping options. Magento 2 Matrix Rates extension by Meetanshi lets your customer choose shipping options based on any variables that the store owner might want to add unlike the default Magento Standard Table Rates.

Customer satisfaction which is very much important can be achieved by facilitating the best-suited shipping options with the help of Meetanshi’s Magento 2 Matrix Rates extension.

Benefits of Choosing Meetanshi's Magento 2 Matrix Rates:

  • Location-based Rates

  • Ability to add custom attributes related to all types of products for best Shipping Rates

  • One can add Product Attributes using .csv file

  • Allows Maximum Flexibility and Reliability for Shipping Rates

  • Lets you offer Shipping Options based on the Product Quantity

  • Allow making Shipping Options dependable on Weight of the Products

  • Option to differ Shipping Options based on Product Price

  • Display Estimated Time & Date along with Each Shipping Method

  • Supports Shipping Rates either in Percentage or at a Flat Rate

  • Differ Shipping Rates for Specific Product Types &amp; Groups

  • Virtual Product Type Excluded from the Shipping Rates

  • Assign Maximum and Minimum Shipping Rates to be applied on whole order with multiple products
