Magento 2 Payment Restrictions

Magento 2 Payment Restrictions Extension helps you restrict Payment Methods based on your needs.

    Organized payment management is the core of every business, be it online or offline. To assist Magento 2 store owners in this task, Meetanshi has come up with Magento 2 Payment Restrictions extension. It allows to effectively manage payments in your Magento 2 store. Customize payment rules with this extension and create a flawless payment system. 

    The feature-rich extension allows a specific payment gateway to trusted customers as well as restrict to some particular group of users. You can restrict payment methods based on product attributes like color, size. For example, for a specific product, you can restrict “Cash on Delivery”.

    Provide more convenience in shopping experience to the loyal customers and hence benefit your store. Use Magento 2 Payment Restrictions to do so.

    Benefits of Magento 2 Payment Fee:

    • Allows creating Flexible Payment Rules.
    • Use Product Attributes to Restrict Payment Method.
    • Allows setting Payment limitations based on Shipping Parameters.
    • Enables admin to restrict payment methods based on the shopping behavior of customer groups.
    • Use Customer Attributes to Restrict Payment Methods.
    • Restrict payments based on store views.
