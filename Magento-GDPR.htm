Magento GDPR

Magento GDPR extension by Meetanshi helps to comply your Magento store with EU's GDPR law.

GDPR i.e, General Data Protection Regulation governs what and how an E-commerce store handles customers' personal details. The GDPR law imposes the store owners to handle the customer data carefully. It allows consumers to control the data, check or modify it, or even delete it. To be in compliance with GDPR, Magento store owners need to take action as the online consumers of Magento store comprises of EU's citizens too. Hence, Meetanshi has developed Magento GDPR extension to let the admin implement the changes in the store to make it compliant with GDPR.

The module takes care of your privacy policy, cookie policy, and sees to it that your Magneto store is compliant with EU's GDPR. It allows customers to control their data, modify or delete it. Such a facility will make you win your customers' trust!

Benefits of Meetanshi's Magento GDPR

  • Enable or disable the GDPR module from the backend.
  • The extension is in line with EU's GDPR legislative requirements.
  • Create or modify the privacy policy of Magento store according to GDPR or any local rules.
  • Notify the customers at a time in mass, about the updated privacy policy and ask for their consent in a single click.
  • Privacy policy compliance on checkout/registration pages.
  • Supports backend grid for privacy policy replies.
  • A customer can download their personal data in form of a CSV file, request to delete the account or anonymize their data.
  • Send Emails to users to request their consent regarding the new or existing privacy policy.
  • Admin can monitor all the actions related to privacy policy consent and account information performed by users.
  • Option to export users data based on provided privacy policy consents.
