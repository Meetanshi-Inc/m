Magento 2 EBS Payment Gateway

Magento 2 EBS Payment Gateway by Meetanshi offers secure integration of Magento 2 and EBS payment gateway.

The Magento 2 EBS Payment Gateway extension by Meetanshi integrates Magento 2 with the EBS payment gateway. 

The facility of secure online payments in a Magento 2 store is a must. Additionally, features like multi-currency support and multiple channels to accept payments are beneficial. The module has it all!

With the Magento 2 EBS Payment Gateway module, capture online payments through net banking, credit and debit cards. Offer a safe platform for online shopping to Magento 2 store customers, hence, enhancing the customer experience!

Benefits of Meetanshi's Magento 2 EBS Payment Gateway

  • The extension requires PCI DSS 3.0 compliance.
  • Accept online payments via debit card, credit card, and international cards such as Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover & JCB.
  • Offers test environment.
  • Leverage the support for multiple currencies to access the global customer base.
