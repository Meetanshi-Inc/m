Magento Orange Money Payments

Magento Orange Money Payments by Meetanshi offers secure online payments by easy integration of Magento and Orange Money payment gateway.

The choice of payment gateway to accept online payments in your Magento store impacts the sales to a great extent. However, the integration of a payment gateway with the store requires an expert's hand. To help store owners integrate a popular and widely trusted payment gateway, Orange Money Web Payment, by Orange, Meetanshi has developed the Magento Orange Money Payments extension.

For countries like Ivory Coast and Senegal, the extension offers to accept a secure hosted payment via Orange Money payment gateway. Win customers' trust and boost sales by ensuring a safe payment process in your Magento store.

Magento Orange Money Payments extension supports sandbox mode. Also, it is easy to integrate the payment gateway with Magento using the authorization code and the secret code.

Benefits of Meetanshi's Magento Orange Money Payments

  • Integrates Magento with Orange Money Web Payment for Ivory Coast and Senegal.
  • Ensures security with the hosted online payment method.
  • Show custom title in the frontend for the payment method.
  • Show the payment gateway logo in the checkout page.
  • Offers sandbox mode.
  • Use merchant ID and gateway URLs to integrate the Orange Money Web payment gateway with the Magento store.
  • Only selected countries are allowed to use the payment method.
  • Display additional details on the checkout.
  • The payment is completed on the hosted payment page only after entering the mobile number and getting the secret code.
  • Customers can check the payment details from their "My Account" section.
  • Admin can check the payment details at Sales Order View section at the backend.
  • Secure online payments via a popular payment gateway help win customers' trust.
